Quick Answers

Frequently asked questions

What's your editing turnaround time?

For standard YouTube videos (5–15 min), turnaround is typically 3–5 business days. For longer cinematic projects or promos, it's 7–14 days depending on complexity. Rush delivery is available — mention it in your message.

What formats do you deliver in?

I deliver in H.264 or H.265 MP4 for web/YouTube, ProRes for professional workflows, and any custom format you need. Standard resolutions: 1080p, 4K. Frame rates: 24fps, 30fps, 60fps.

Do you do revisions?

Yes — all packages include at least 2 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions can be added. I aim to get it right the first time by discussing your vision thoroughly before starting.

Can you work with my raw footage?

Absolutely. Send me your raw footage via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or Dropbox. I work with formats from most cameras — DSLR, mirrorless, action cams, and smartphone footage. The more organized your files, the faster the edit.

Do you create content for the BLUdot YouTube channel?

Yes! BLUdot on YouTube is my channel where I post facts, tips, and creative content. The editing skills I offer clients are the same ones you see in my YouTube videos — that's the portfolio.

What's the best way to reach you quickly?

The contact form here is best for project inquiries. For quick questions, DM on Instagram or Twitter. I try to respond to all messages within 24 hours — usually faster. If it's urgent, say so in your message!
